GFA utilizes widely recognized and accepted research techniques designed to withstand potential challenges asserting inappropriate methodology.  Utilizing trained research professionals, GFA conducts studies analyzing up to several thousand respondents to minimize the margin of error and maximize accuracy depending on client's needs.

  • Feasibility Studies 

  • Economic Impact Studies Including Large Scale Surveys

  • Financial Investigation To Help Detect White Collar Crimes

  • Financial Crisis Management

  • Business Valuations

  • Bankruptcy Option Analysis

  • Strategic Market Withdrawal Analysis

  • Financial Data Analysis

  • Business Plan Reviews

  • Market Strength Studies (sample overbuild study)

  • Growth Option Studies

  • Strategic Modeling/Forecasting

Any and/or all the service above can be combined into a comprehensive financial analysis package for a reasonable price and with tangible results.  We stand behind our work and only bill on a value-added basis.

If we cannot help solve problems, we do not bill!  These services can all be coordinated with existing in-house or external counsel to maximize client comfort.
