GFA's risk management services entail analyzing current policies, procedures, practices, training, and actual facility operations and then customizing solutions to meet client needs.  

Risk management services entail more than just delivering a report, but also include detailed and practical assistance in implementing and educating those employees responsible for implementing the solutions. Such "coaching" can range from teaching an in-house course to providing short-term project or program management, or "live fire training" such as training for a potential tragedy by simulating unannounced emergencies.
